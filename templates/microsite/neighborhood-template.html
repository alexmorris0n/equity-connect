What is a Reverse Mortgage?

A reverse mortgage is a loan that allows homeowners 62 and older to convert part of their home equity into cash. Unlike a traditional mortgage, you don't make monthly payments to the lender.

Instead, the loan is repaid when you move, sell the home, or pass away. This can provide significant financial flexibility for homeowners in {{neighborhood_name}}.

Benefits for {{neighborhood_name}} Homeowners

  • No monthly mortgage payments required
  • Access to home equity without selling
  • Funds can be used for any purpose
  • You retain ownership of your home
  • Protected by federal regulations
